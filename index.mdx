# Welcome to UTMStack

UTMStack is a unified threat management platform that combines SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) and XDR (Extended Detection and Response) capabilities. Our platform provides real-time correlation of log data, threat intelligence, and malware activity patterns to identify and halt complex threats.
